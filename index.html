About This Event

Raabta is a celebration of words, emotions, creativity, and expression. It brings together literature enthusiasts, poets, storytellers, speakers, and performers on a single platform to showcase talent, exchange ideas, and build meaningful connections through art and language. The fest aims to inspire young minds, promote cultural dialogue, and nurture creative excellence.

Organized by the Board of Student Publications (BSP), RAABTA brings together students, artists, poets, musicians, and literature enthusiasts from across the country. Join us as we explore the beautiful connection between traditional and contemporary art forms through two days of captivating performances, literary sessions, cultural events, and creative workshops.

Experience the magic of Sufi nights, the rhythm of Sargam, the elegance of Noor Ball, the creativity of fashion shows, and the power of poetry. RAABTA 2026 promises to be an unforgettable celebration of art, culture, and human connections.
